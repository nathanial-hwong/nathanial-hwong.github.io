Case Study: Health Companion Super App

Designing a mobile app to help individuals manage chronic health conditions.

Introduction

Chronic health conditions require consistent management of medications, appointments, and communication with healthcare providers. This case study outlines the design process for the Health Companion Super App. The mobile app's goal is to simplify these tasks and most importantly to help users keep up with their health needs.

The Task

The goal was to design a user-friendly mobile app that allows users to:

  • Set medication reminders
  • Track medication usage
  • Schedule and manage doctor appointments
  • Communicate with healthcare professionals

Personas

Based on user research, three personas were created to represent the target users:

The Independant Teenager

The Busy Dad

The Forgetful Senior

User Journey Map

The user journey map allows developpers to identify steps in the user process that can cause inconveniences for the user. This helps identify situations which might require more research or improvement.
